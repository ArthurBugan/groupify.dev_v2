# Saregama Music: How Old Bollywood Built a 59M Subscriber Empire

Saregama Music isn’t just another Indian music channel—it’s a **YouTube algorithm mastermind** with 59M subscribers. Their secret? **Nostalgia-driven content and regional expansion**.

---

## The Classic Bollywood Machine

Saregama’s success comes from:
1. **Old Movie Songs**: Nostalgia goldmine
2. **Regional Music**: Tamil/Telugu/Punjabi hits
3. **Data-Driven Playlists**: Algorithm-optimized

| Content Type | View Performance | Monetization Potential |
| :--- | :---: | :---: |
| Classic Songs | 5M–30M views | Highest ad RPM (nostalgia) |
| Regional Hits | 2M–15M views | Evergreen content |
| Compilations | 1M–10M views | Playlist gold |

### Why It Works
- **Zero Competition**: Most labels ignore old Bollywood
- **High Retention**: Nostalgia drives watch time
- **Ad Revenue Goldmine**: Lower CPMs but higher watch time

## The Algorithm Hacks

Saregama doesn’t just upload music—they **engineer virality**:

- **"Trending Now" Playlists**:
  - Curated based on real-time YouTube data
  - Drives 30% of their traffic
- **Thumbnail Psychology**:
  - Celebrity close-ups + bold text
  - A/B tested for CTR (2.5% average)
- **Shorts Strategy**:
  - 15-second hooks from full songs
  - 1M+ daily views

## Monetization Beyond Ads

Saregama earns through:
1. **YouTube Ad Revenue**: $20M/year (estimated)
