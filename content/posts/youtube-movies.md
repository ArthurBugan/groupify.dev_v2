# YouTube Movies: The Streaming Giant’s Film Hub

YouTube Movies is the **auto-generated powerhouse** of Hollywood and regional cinema, curating trailers, clips, and full films.

## Key Strategy
- **Algorithm-driven playlists** (e.g., "Trending Now," "Bollywood Hits").
- **Zero production cost**—relies on studio partnerships.
- **Global reach** via YouTube’s ad network.
