# YouTube SAB: How Sony Entertainment Turned TV Clips into a 98M Subscriber Empire

YouTube SAB (Sony SAB) isn’t just a channel—it’s **proof that TV and digital can coexist**. With 98M subscribers, they’ve mastered **content repurposing** to dominate Indian YouTube.

---

## The TV-to-YouTube Playbook

SAB’s strategy revolves around:
1. **Clip-Based Content**:
   - 2–3 min highlights from TV shows
   - Edited for mobile viewing
2. **Regional Expansion**:
   - Tamil, Telugu, Kannada versions
3. **Algorithm Optimization**:
   - SEO titles + thumbnails

| Show Type | YouTube Adaptation | View Performance |
| :--- | :--- | :---: |
| Comedy Shows | "Best Jokes" compilations | 5M–20M views |
| Reality TV | "Drama Moments" edits | 3M–15M views |
| Movie Promos | Trailer + making-of | 10M+ for blockbusters |

### Why It Works
- **Zero Production Cost**: Uses existing footage
- **High Retention**: Short clips maximize watch time
- **Ad-Friendly**: Perfect mid-roll placement

## Monetization Through Volume

SAB’s ad strategy relies on:
- **High Upload Frequency**: 30+ clips daily
- **Mid-Roll Ads**: Perfect for 2–3 min videos
- **Brand Deals**: TV show sponsors extend to YouTube

### RPM Optimization Tricks
- **Short-Form Content** = more ad slots per hour
- **TV-Style Pacing** = lower bounce rates than vloggers
