## The "Beast" Business Model: Goals & Future

MrBeast's goals extend far beyond YouTube views. He is building a brand that exists in the physical world:

### 1. Retention is King
Jimmy's primary goal is 100% retention. He analyzes every frame to ensure viewers never click away. This "fast-paced" editing style has become the industry standard.

<ins class="adsbygoogle">
</ins>

### 2. Global Domination (Multi-Language)
He was a pioneer in using YouTube's multi-audio track feature. By dubbing his videos into 10+ languages, he effectively multiplied his potential audience by the global population.

### 3. Philanthropy as a Brand
His goal is to make "giving away money" a sustainable business model. The more he gives, the more people watch; the more people watch, the more he can give.
